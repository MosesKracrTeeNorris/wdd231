NP Membership Benefits

Designed for government and educational institutions and non-profit organizations.

  • Free listing in the online directory.
  • Access to monthly newsletter.
  • Discounted attendance to certain workshops.

Bronze Membership Benefits

The entry-level tier for small and new businesses.

  • All NP benefits.
  • 5% discount on event tickets.
  • Access to member-only business seminars.
  • Basic social media shoutout once per quarter.

Silver Membership Benefits

A step up for businesses ready to increase visibility and network.

  • All Bronze benefits.
  • 10% discount on event tickets.
  • Listing in 1 category in the print directory.
  • Eligibility for member-exclusive training programs.

Gold Membership Benefits

The premium tier offering maximum resources and exposure opportunities.

  • All Silver benefits.
  • 20% discount on event tickets.
  • Featured Gold Spotlight on the chamber homepage (random rotation).
  • Quarterly feature article in the newsletter.
  • Priority access to sponsorship opportunities.
